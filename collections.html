Tote Collection
The PrintTote Collection
Original Art · Sustainable Canvas · Artisan Discovery
What This Is

Authentic art forms — Pattachitra, Madhubani, Kalamkari, Gond — faithfully reproduced on sustainable canvas tote bags. Each print originates from an original hand-painted work by one of our artisans, carrying their name, their region, and their craft tradition forward into everyday life.

The Purpose

A tote is a passport. Every bag that leaves our studio introduces the artisan's name and art form to a new set of eyes — making each purchase an act of artisan discovery as much as a personal statement of taste.

Painted Collection
The PaintedCollection
Premium · Custom Hand-Painted · On Request
What This Is

These are works of art in the truest sense — custom hand-painted pieces commissioned directly from our artisans. Some works are available online for immediate enquiry; others exist only as digital previews in the Artist Gallery, waiting for the right collector to bring them to life.

How to Commission a Piece

Visit the Artist Gallery, browse each artisan's digital portfolio, and select the work that speaks to you. Place a custom order request — the artisan will personally confirm receipt, accept the commission, and let you know the delivery timeline. All communication and fulfilment is coordinated by Lahe Lahe.
