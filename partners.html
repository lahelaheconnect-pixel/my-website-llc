Artisanal Partner
ArtisanalCraft Makers
Painters · Weavers · Traditional Art Forms
Who This Is For

You are a painter, weaver, or practitioner of a traditional Indian art form — Pattachitra, Madhubani, Kalamkari, Gond, Warli, or beyond. You work with your hands, carry a lineage, and want your craft to reach collectors who truly value it.

How to Apply

Send us your portfolio as a PDF, including images of your art forms and a brief note — 100 words or fewer — about yourself and why you want to partner with Lahe Lahe Collectives. Please also include at least one physical or digital sample of your work. We review every submission with care.

Join as Artist →
Collaboration Partner
Co-brand &Collaboration
Gifting · Events · Corporate · Co-branded Merch
Who This Is For

You represent a gifting house, an event brand, a corporate gifting programme, or a company that believes in beautiful, story-led branding. We offer co-branded merchandise — from art-printed totes to QR-enabled collectibles carrying your identity alongside ours, and even the option to add your company logo to curated pieces from our collection.

How to Start a Conversation

Write to us with a brief on your brand, the occasion or campaign you have in mind, and the scale of the collaboration (units, timeline, budget range). The most exciting partnerships begin with a single honest line about what you're trying to create.

Retail & Spaces
Cafés &Curated Shops
Retail · Online Stores · Craft-Centric Venues
Who This Is For

You run a café, a concept store, a handmade goods boutique, or an online shop that believes in aesthetic and craft-led commerce. If your space or platform naturally attracts people who appreciate slow, deliberate beauty — we'd love to find a way to live there too.

How to Begin

Tell us a little about your space or platform — what you stock, who your customers are, and how you imagine Lahe Lahe fitting in. Whether it's a consignment arrangement, a curated corner, or a pop-up, we're open to what makes sense for both of us.
