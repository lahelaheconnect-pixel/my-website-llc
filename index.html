The Digital Artisan Bridge

Every Tote Tells a Living Story

Scan the QR code on the back of any Lahe Lahe tote to meet the artisan who painted it — their name, village, craft tradition, and the time they devoted to your piece.

The Artisan Bridge — Back of Tote
01
The Artisan Creates
Master craftspeople from rural Odisha, Bihar & Andhra Pradesh hand-paint each piece using centuries-old techniques — no machines, no prints, no shortcuts.
02
We Curate & Document
Each creation is catalogued with the artisan's name, village, tradition, and story — then embedded as a QR code on the back of every tote.
03
You Connect & Carry
Scan the QR to meet the hands that made your piece. Your purchase directly funds their workshop, skill development, and community.
